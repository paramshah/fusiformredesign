What are Orthotics?

Lower extremity orthoses are externally applied devices that help balance the biomechanics of your feet and legs, correcting walking gait and preventing muscle deformities. They are used in treatment for Cerebral Palsy and other muscular diseases to increase strength, comfort and independence.
